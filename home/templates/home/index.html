Wildlife-friendly
How can I make my garden more wild life friendly?

Transforming your garden into a wildlife-friendly sanctuary is easier than you think! By incorporating native plants, creating shelter, and providing food sources like nectar-rich flowers and bird feeders, you can attract butterflies, bees, and birds. Avoid pesticides, add a small water feature, and let parts of your garden grow naturally to support biodiversity. A thriving ecosystem starts with small changes, making your green space a haven for nature!
